How I work

Simple, transparent, pay-per-progress.

No surprise invoices. You know what you're paying for at every stage.

STEP 01

Scope the project

We talk through what you need. Clearly defined tasks get a flat quote; anything uncertain (like debugging someone else's code) gets scoped separately once I've reviewed it.

STEP 02

Fixed quote, no guesswork

You get a rate for the defined work upfront. I accept real money (preferred) or Robux — Robux payments cover the platform tax.

STEP 03

Pay-per-progress

Payment tracks delivery — 20% done means 20% paid. You always see progress before paying for it.

Payment Flexibility: While I highly prefer a pay-per-progress structure to keep things transparent for both of us, I am open to alternative payment milestones depending on the scope of the project. We can discuss what works best for your budget during scoping.
Note on debugging: Fixing existing code is billed based on actual time and complexity after I've reviewed it, since it's the one part of a project that's hard to estimate blind. I'll always flag this upfront before it affects a deadline.
